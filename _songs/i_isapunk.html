We did a version of this in 1986, which was fairly sedate, and used the VL-Tone and a couple of tin whistles!!!!! So when we came to do this version in 1987 on setup 3, we wanted it to be a bit more energetic!!!!

We also wanted to use a guitar, but we didn't have a proper "punk" guitar, so PritStik Mik made a guitar noise using his "vocoder", with loads of feedback, then sampled it and used it on the VSS100 in hyperspeed mode, and hey presto- punk!!!

Add to that PritStik Mej on the tin whistle adding some extra "feedback", and you have the perfect setting for our classic punky lyrics!! (BTW Hope you like the "Granny Bates" joke!!!! We certainly fell about on the floor when we wrote it!!!!!)
