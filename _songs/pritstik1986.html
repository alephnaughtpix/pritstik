This is the earliest PritStik recording that survives to this day!!!! And it's the earliest version of our theme song!!! Technically it should be called "PritStik (1986 B)", but we lost the original "1986 A" recording!!!! (Sniff sniff!!!)

Anyway, you can hear the amazing energy of our early setup 1 recordings on this rare gem!!! Believe it or not, the only overdubs are the drum solos and the extra guitars near the end!!! The rest was recorded live with one microphone strapped to PritStik Mik's funky Casio VL-Tone, and the other capturing PritStik Mej's energetic drumming, and PritStik's unbridled vocal style!!!!

In fact, it's so unbridled that at one point PritStik Mik announces the "drum solo" when he really means the "guitar solo"!!!! And believe it or not, the "guitar solo" is not really done by a guitar here either!!! It's PritStik Mik through a vocoder!!!!!! Also listen out for a radio being tuned halfway through the drum solo!!!!

That's the kind of wacky off the wall people we are!!!!
